About Us

Br Accounting and Consulting Services, Inc. and Br Insurance and financial group, Inc. is a company that has been assisting a variety of professional accounting payroll, personal or commercial insurance services.

We are specialists in accounting, payroll and insurance with over 25 years of experience. We pride ourselves in providing our clients with a professional service, guaranteed accuracy, excellent customer service, and the highest level of confidentiality.
