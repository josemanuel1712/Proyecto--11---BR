Br Accounting and Consulting Services, Inc. and Br Insurance and financial group, Inc. is a company that has been assisting a variety of professional accounting payroll, personal or commercial insurance services.

On March 14, 2005 Equity One Insurance Agency, LLC was recognized as a legal entity Organized. The company purpose was to function as a full-service insurance provider offering property, casualty, life, and health insurance products to both individual and commercial customers.

To aid in the strategy of building a nationwide brand name, Equity One Insurance Agency, LLC began operating under the name of Global Green Insurance Agency on August 27, 2007.
